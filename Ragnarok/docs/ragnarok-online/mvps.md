## Spawn Mechanics

### Normal Monsters
- Fixed count per map (set by server spawn files)
- Respawn immediately upon death (delay = 0) or with short delay (1–5s)
- Random position within designated spawn area

### Mini-Bosses
- `boss_monster` type with fixed respawn timer
- Typically 60–120 minute respawn
- Single spawn per map
- Some have variable spawn: `delay + random(0, variance)`

### MVPs
- `boss_monster` type with longer fixed timer + variance
- Spawn: `base_delay + random(0, variance)` after death
- At server start: spawn at random position within designated area
- After death: tombstone appears at death location (shows killer name and time)
- Only one instance per map
- Typical ranges: 60–120 min (most), 300 min (Tao Gunka), 30–60 min (Pharaoh, Dark Lord, Orc Hero)

### MVP Competition
- The player who deals the **most total damage** receives:
  1. MVP EXP bonus (separate from party share)
  2. MVP item drops (3 special drop slots with their own rates)
  3. MVP announcement to all players on the map
- Non-MVP players who participated still get normal EXP/drops from the kill
