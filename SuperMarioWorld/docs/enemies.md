# Super Mario World — Enemy Catalog

Companion to [SPEC.md](../SPEC.md) §7. All enemies from the SNES original (1990).

---

## Ground Enemies

| Enemy | HP | Behavior | Defeat Methods | Notes |
|-------|-----|----------|----------------|-------|
| Galoomba | 1 stomp to flip, then kick | Walks forward, falls off edges | Stomp (flips over, kickable), fireball, cape, shell, star | Not traditional Goombas — round, cannot be killed by a single stomp alone |
| Koopa Troopa (Green) | 1 | Walks forward, falls off edges | Stomp → shell; fireball → coin; cape; star | Shell can be kicked or carried |
| Koopa Troopa (Red) | 1 | Walks forward, turns at edges | Same as Green | Safer patrol pattern |
| Koopa Troopa (Yellow) | 1 | Walks forward; when re-shelled, kicks shell at Mario | Same as Green | Unique re-shell behavior |
| Koopa Troopa (Blue) | 1 | Walks forward; when re-shelled, chases Mario at high speed | Same as Green | Most dangerous Koopa variant |
| Koopa Paratroopa (Green) | 1 stomp → Koopa | Bounces in arcs | Stomp removes wings → becomes Koopa Troopa | |
| Koopa Paratroopa (Red) | 1 stomp → Koopa | Flies in set horizontal/vertical pattern | Stomp removes wings → becomes Koopa Troopa | |
| Paragaloomba | 1 stomp → Galoomba | Hops along ground with small wings | Stomp removes wings → becomes Galoomba | |
| Parachute Galoomba | 1 | Floats down on parachute, then walks | All standard methods | Parachute detaches on landing |
| Rex | 2 stomps | Blue dinosaur, walks toward Mario | 1st stomp squishes flat (still active), 2nd kills; fireball ×1; cape ×1 | Cape and fireball are one-hit kills |
| Chargin' Chuck | 3 stomps / 5 fireballs | Football-player Koopa, 6 behavior variants | 3 stomps; 5 fireballs; 1 cape hit; star | Variants: running, jumping, kicking footballs, throwing baseballs, digging rocks, splitting into 3. Hybrid: 2 fireballs + 1 stomp also defeats. |
| Monty Mole | 1 | Pops from ground/hills, chases Mario | Stomp, fireball, cape, star, shell | Emerges when Mario approaches |
| Mega Mole | Cannot be defeated | Huge mole with sunglasses, walks slowly | Invincible to all attacks | Can be ridden on top of as a platform |
| Wiggler | 1 stomp to enrage | Caterpillar, walks slowly | 1st stomp turns red and speeds up; star, Yoshi swallow | Cannot be defeated by normal means after enraging (star/Yoshi only) |
| Pokey | 4 segments | 4-segment cactus, walks toward Mario | Fireball removes segments; cape; star; Yoshi eats segments | Each fireball removes one segment |
| Ninji | 1 | Bouncing star-shaped enemy | Stomp, fireball, cape, star | Appears in castles and Special Zone |
| Dino Rhino | 1 stomp → Dino-Torch | Large blue triceratops | Stomp → becomes Dino-Torch; fireball ×1; cape ×1 | |
| Dino-Torch | 1 | Small fire-breathing dinosaur | Stomp, fireball, cape, star | Breathes short-range fire periodically |
| Muncher | Star only | Black chomper plant, stationary | Star; all other attacks fail | Spin jump bounces safely off them |
| Bob-omb | 1 stomp to activate | Walks, then explodes after timer | Stomp starts fuse; can be picked up and thrown; fireball; cape | Explodes a few seconds after being stomped |
| Parabomb | 1 | Bob-omb descending on parachute | All standard methods | Parachute detaches on contact |
| Buzzy Beetle | 1 stomp → shell | Walks forward, turns at edges | Stomp → shell (fireball-immune); cape; star | Immune to fireballs; shell is kickable |
| Spike Top | Cannot be stomped | Buzzy Beetle with spike on shell | Cape, star, Yoshi; fireball-immune | Spike prevents stomping; spin jump bounces off safely |
| Bony Beetle | Regenerates | Skeletal beetle, walks back and forth | Stomp collapses temporarily; regenerates after ~5 seconds; star | Periodically extends spikes (invulnerable during spike phase) |
| Sumo Bro | Cannot be stomped from above | Sumo wrestler on high platforms | Cape, star; cannot reach normally | Stomps platform to create lightning → ground fire below |

## Post-Special-Zone Cosmetic Replacements

After completing all 8 Special Zone levels, the following sprite swaps occur (behavior unchanged):

| Original | Replacement | Visual Change |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Koopa Troopa / Paratroopa | Mask Koopa | Wears Mario mask instead of shell |
| Piranha Plant | Pumpkin Plant | Jack-o'-lantern head |
| Jumping Piranha Plant | Jumping Pumpkin Plant | Jack-o'-lantern head |
| Bullet Bill | Pidgit Bill | Small black bird sprite |
| Green Koopas | Recolored to Yellow | Palette swap |
| Red Koopas | Recolored to Blue | Palette swap |
