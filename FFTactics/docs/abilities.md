## Mime (ものまね士)

Mime has no learnable action abilities. It automatically copies the last action performed by any allied unit, executing the same ability at the same target area for free (no MP, no CT).

**Limitations:**
- Cannot mimic other Mimes
- Cannot mimic monster abilities
- Cannot mimic some unique character abilities
- Cannot mimic Item usage
- Cannot equip any abilities, equipment, or secondary action sets
- Inherits outstanding raw stats: highest HP/Speed/PA/MA multipliers among generic jobs
