## Sub-Stat Roll Values (5-Star Artifacts)

Each sub-stat roll has four possible tiers. The tiers correspond to approximately 70%, 80%, 90%, and 100% of the maximum roll value.

|----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| Flat HP | 209.13 | 239.00 | 268.88 | 298.75 |
| Flat ATK | 13.62 | 15.56 | 17.51 | 19.45 |
| Flat DEF | 16.20 | 18.52 | 20.83 | 23.15 |
| HP% | 4.08% | 4.66% | 5.25% | 5.83% |
| ATK% | 4.08% | 4.66% | 5.25% | 5.83% |
| DEF% | 5.10% | 5.83% | 6.56% | 7.29% |

**Constraint:** A sub-stat cannot duplicate the artifact's main stat. E.g., an ATK% Sands cannot roll ATK% as a sub-stat.

## Artifact Leveling

### Maximum Levels by Rarity

| Rarity | Max Level | Sub-Stat Upgrade Levels |
|--------|-----------|------------------------|
| 1-Star | +4 | +4 |
| 2-Star | +4 | +4 |
| 3-Star | +12 | +4, +8, +12 |
| 4-Star | +16 | +4, +8, +12, +16 |
| 5-Star | +20 | +4, +8, +12, +16, +20 |

### Sub-Stat Upgrade Rules

- A 5-star artifact starts with either 3 or 4 sub-stats.
- At each sub-stat upgrade level (+4, +8, +12, +16, +20), one sub-stat is upgraded:
  - If the artifact has fewer than 4 sub-stats, a new random sub-stat is added (from the pool, excluding duplicates of existing subs and the main stat).
  - If the artifact already has 4 sub-stats, one existing sub-stat is randomly selected and increased by a random roll from the tier table above.
- A 5-star artifact with 4 starting sub-stats receives 5 upgrade rolls total (at +4, +8, +12, +16, +20), all into existing sub-stats.
- A 5-star artifact with 3 starting sub-stats receives 1 new sub-stat at +4, then 4 upgrade rolls into existing sub-stats at +8, +12, +16, +20.
