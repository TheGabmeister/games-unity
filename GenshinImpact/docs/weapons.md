### Weapons NOT in v1.0 (Added Later)

The following 5-star weapons were NOT available at v1.0 launch despite sometimes being associated with launch:
- **Memory of Dust** (Catalyst) -- added v1.1
- **Vortex Vanquisher** (Polearm) -- added v1.1
- **The Unforged** (Claymore) -- added v1.1
- **Summit Shaper** (Sword) -- added v1.2

The following 4-star weapons were added post-launch:
- **Snow-Tombed Starsilver** (Claymore) -- v1.2 (Dragonspine)
- **Frostbearer** (Catalyst) -- v1.2 (Dragonspine)
- **Festering Desire** (Sword) -- v1.2 event weapon
- **Dragonspine Spear** (Polearm) -- v1.2
- **Lithic Blade** (Claymore) -- v1.3
- **Lithic Spear** (Polearm) -- v1.3
- **The Alley Flash** (Sword) -- v1.4
- **Alley Hunter** (Bow) -- v1.4
- **Wine and Song** (Catalyst) -- v1.4
- **Windblume Ode** (Bow) -- v1.4 event weapon
- **Dodoco Tales** (Catalyst) -- v1.6 event weapon
