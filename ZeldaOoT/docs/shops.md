|--------|--------|

## Income Sources

- Cutting grass, breaking pots, opening chests
- Defeating enemies (some drop Rupees)
- Selling items: Bugs (50), Fish (100), Big Poe (50 points toward bottle reward), Poe Souls (variable)
- Treasure Chest Game (Castle Town, 10 Rupees to play; up to 50 Rupee prize)
- Minigame prizes
- Silver Rupee puzzles in dungeons (5 each, used as keys)
